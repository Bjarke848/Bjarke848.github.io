1. Introduction

CogniMate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our intelligent health and performance companion application and services.

CogniMate is designed with privacy-first principles. We believe you should have complete sovereignty over your health and activity data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health and Activity Data

With your explicit consent, we may collect and process:

  • Sleep Data: Sleep cycles, duration, quality metrics, and sleep stages
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): HRV measurements and trends
  • Activity Data: Steps, workouts, exercise sessions, and movement patterns
  • Recovery Metrics: Stress levels, recovery scores, and fatigue indicators
  • Physiological Data: Heart rate, body temperature, and other biometric indicators

2.2 Calendar and Schedule Data

To provide intelligent scheduling features, we may access:

  • Calendar events and appointments
  • Meeting schedules and availability
  • Task lists and productivity data
  • Time zone and location information for scheduling

2.3 Environmental Context Data

To enhance our contextual recommendations:

  • Weather information for your location
  • Time zone and general location data
  • Travel patterns and context

2.4 Technical Data

  • Device information and operating system
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Error logs and performance data (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve CogniMate's services:

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Personalized Recommendations: Analyze your health patterns to suggest optimal scheduling and activities
  • Intelligent Planning: Combine biometric data with calendar information for smart agenda management
  • Health Insights: Provide actionable insights based on your sleep, recovery, and activity patterns
  • Context-Aware Suggestions: Incorporate environmental factors into our recommendations

3.2 AI and Machine Learning

  • Train personalized models to improve recommendation accuracy
  • Analyze patterns to predict optimal performance windows
  • Generate contextual insights through our GPT-powered agent

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4.1 Third-Party Integrations

We integrate with the following services with your explicit consent:

  • Garmin Health API: To sync your fitness and health data
  • Apple HealthKit: To access iOS health data
  • Microsoft Graph API: For calendar integration
  • Weather Services: For environmental context
  • OpenAI/GPT Services: For AI-powered insights (data is processed securely)

4.2 Data Processing Partners

We may share limited, necessary data with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Google Cloud) for secure data storage
  • AI processing services for generating insights (with data encryption)
  • Analytics services for app performance (anonymized data only)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Security and Storage

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Local-First Architecture: Where possible, data is processed locally on your device
  • OAuth2 Authentication: Secure authentication for third-party integrations

5.2 Data Retention

  • Health and activity data: Retained as long as your account is active, or as needed for service provision
  • Calendar data: Processed in real-time, minimal retention
  • Usage analytics: Anonymized data retained for service improvement

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

6.2 Consent Management

  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time
  • Manage integration permissions through app settings
  • Opt-out of specific data processing activities

7. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Other legally approved transfer mechanisms

8. Children's Privacy

CogniMate is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • In-app notifications for significant changes

Your continued use of CogniMate after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Regulatory Compliance

CogniMate complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including:

  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (EU)
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act (US)
  • HIPAA: Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (where applicable)
  • PIPEDA: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (Canada)

Data Protection Principles

"You are the product manager of your biology. CogniMate is the assistant."

This philosophy guides everything we do. Your health data belongs to you, and we are merely the custodians helping you derive insights from it.
